# Pricing — Back-Office Automation for SMBs

## Tiers

### Quickwin — $3,500

One process audit + 2 automations + dashboard. ~10 business days.

### Operations Pack — $8,500

Up to 5 automations, owner dashboard, team training, 30-day support.

### Fractional Ops — $2,200/mo

Ongoing automation buildout + monthly review (3-month minimum).

## Payment terms

- **Fixed-price tiers:** 50% on contract signing, 50% on delivery.
- **Monthly retainers:** invoiced on the 1st, Net-15.
- Accepted: ACH, wire, check (Stripe / Mercury). Card on request (3% surcharge).
- All prices in USD. Travel quoted separately if on-site work is needed beyond a 30-mile radius of Huntersville, NC.

## What changes the price

- **Up:** rush timelines (<1 week start), evening/weekend work, multi-stakeholder approval chains, unusual compliance requirements.
- **Down:** flexible timeline, repeat client, willingness to be a public case study.

## Discovery call

Always free, 30 minutes, no obligation. Book by opening an [Engagement Inquiry](../../issues/new?template=engagement-inquiry.yml).

## FAQ

**Will you sign an NDA?** Yes — mutual NDA, our template or yours, before any sensitive details are shared.

**Do you subcontract?** Sometimes, with disclosure. Lead engineering is always John.

**What if the engagement runs long?** Fixed-price tiers stay fixed unless scope changes via change order. Retainers prorate.

**Refunds?** If we miss a written deliverable, we either fix it free or refund that line item. No quibbling.
