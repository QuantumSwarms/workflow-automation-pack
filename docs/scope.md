# Scope — Back-Office Automation for SMBs

## In scope

- Process audit: shadow your team for one day, map the top 5 manual handoffs.
- Build automations in your existing stack (Zapier, Make, n8n, or native integrations).
- Custom glue code (Cloudflare Worker or Google Apps Script) only where SaaS connectors fall short.
- Owner dashboard (Notion, Google Sheet, or simple web page).
- Team training session + written runbook.

## Out of scope

- Replacing your existing SaaS (we automate what you have).
- Bookkeeping or accounting work itself.
- ERP-level implementations.

## Change requests

Anything outside the above is handled via a written change order. We will not start unscoped work without one — protects you from surprise invoices and protects us from scope creep.

## Assumptions

- Decision-maker is reachable within 1 business day during the engagement.
- All access (logins, admin invites) provided within the first 48 hours of kickoff.
- Engagement is paused — not extended — if access is delayed beyond 5 business days. Day count resumes when access is restored.

## Acceptance criteria

Each deliverable in [`deliverables.md`](./deliverables.md) has a checkbox. Engagement is complete when all are checked off and you have signed the handoff doc. Optional 30-day warranty applies to anything we built that regresses without you changing it.
